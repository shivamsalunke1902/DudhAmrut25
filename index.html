The Problem We Solve

India produces 23% of global milk, yet faces critical issues that threaten public health and farmer livelihoods.

Rampant Adulteration

68% of samples are unsafe (FSSAI, 2022). This directly threatens public health and consumer trust.

No Traceability

The origin of contamination is untraceable in the current opaque supply chain, making recalls impossible.

High Spoilage

~20% of milk is wasted due to poor cold chain management and lack of real-time temperature monitoring.

Major Health Risks

An estimated 57% of people are affected by milk-borne diseases, a direct result of poor quality control.

Low FSSAI Compliance

Dairies struggle with the manual, paper-based tracking required for FSSAI traceability norms.

Consumer Distrust

Consumers have no access to verified quality data, leading to widespread distrust in packaged milk.

Our Solution:

DudhAmrut is an IoT and Blockchain-powered platform providing end-to-end traceability and real-time quality monitoring of milk. Our system ensures every drop from the farm to the consumer is safe, pure, and verified.

System Architecture

A high-level overview of our data flow, from farm to consumer.

1. Data Collection

IoT sensors test milk & farmer data is collected.

2. Secure Gateway

Data is sent to the cloud for processing & analysis.

3. Blockchain Storage

Verified data is stored immutably on the Blockchain.

4. Consumer Access

Consumers scan a QR code to see the full, trusted report.
